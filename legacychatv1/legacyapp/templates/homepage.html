Dear beta tester We are thrilled to extend this invitation to you to join our beta testing program for our latest software. Your willingness to volunteer your time and provide valuable feedback is greatly appreciated by our entire team. As a beta tester, your insights are crucial in shaping the final version of our product. Here are the key things to observe during the testing phase:

  1. First and foremost, does the text produced by the AI engine is of a quality that will satisfy your needs? Or in other words, is the effort of answering the questions worthwhile?
  2. The second question: is the product easy to use, is it intuitive where the next move makes sense?
  3. Did you find errors, labels or names that do not match the function?
  4. Considering the product function (after fixing the errors) how much will you pay for a software application that produces an autobiography of this quality after the effort of answering 120 questions?

To answer the questionnaire, you don’t have to complete the 20 chapters, answering 5 questions of the first chapter, and processing them to a final draft will be sufficient. It should not take more than an hour. But of course if you like the result of your effort, continue and enjoy it. We will contact you in four weeks to have a phone interview about your views on the four points mentioned above.
